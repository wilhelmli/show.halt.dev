LI WILHELM HAOJIANG 李镐江

Inspired by pop culture and advertisements, Li combines modern technology with traditional methods of art production such as source collages.
李镐江的创作冲动来源于现代与经典的流行文化与广告。他利用人工智能与滤镜对参考素材进行数码再创作。
