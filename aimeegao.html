AIMEE GAO 高雅

Aimee Gao is new generation of artists, her paintings are retro and gorgeous like a complex expression of the mystery, seemingly recording people's dreams, but in fact expressing the phenomenology of the world.
她的画作复古绚丽像谜一样的复杂表达，看似记录着人们的梦境，实则表述着大千世界种种现象学。
