海湾风云KRAXY BAJA｜电矩狂魔CTON特别活动

联合 KRAXY BAJA，电矩狂魔CTON 为 KRAXY BAJA 的忠实粉丝共同策划其最后一期海边派对。

作为拥有长期派对策划历史的KRAXY BAJA，自2012年起便拥有广泛的外国友人参与，为远在他乡的拼搏者们带来了一个又一个难忘的派对。

在这次温馨的告别，我们将搭乘 电矩列车（CTON EXPRESS），重返中国境内最大的沙滩俱乐部—MOOON BEACH CLUB，一起追溯那个欢声笑语、莫逆之交的美好时代。

CTON and KRAXY BAJA are excited to announce a farewell party from MARCH 31st to APRIL 1st, for KRAXY BAJA's most loyal fans.

KRAXY BAJA, a sophisticated collective of amazing musicians. Since 2012, KRAXY BAJA parties have become a welcoming family shelter for expats, creating numerous unforgettable memories, celebrating the tears and joys of those far far away from home.

At this epic farewell, people are brought together again at MOOON BEACH CLUB, the largest beach club in China, salute to the beautiful friendships of an era of great wonders and dreams.
